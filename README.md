# Predictur: Tourism Demand Forecasting

**A data intelligence system for the Colombian Caribbean leveraging data engineering and time series to optimize strategic decision-making.**

---

## 📝 Introduction
Tourism is the economic engine of the Colombian Caribbean. However, many critical decisions in the sector are often made without sufficient quantitative evidence. **Predictur** is designed to determine if it is possible to anticipate demand with precision through an end-to-end system that combines automated data extraction, advanced predictive models, and clear visual storytelling.

This project integrates three fundamental disciplines:
1. **Data Engineering:** To ensure reliable and traceable data collection.
2. **Time Series:** To extract historical patterns and generate actionable forecasts.
3. **Data Storytelling:** To transform complex numbers into strategic recommendations for decision-makers.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Automated Data Pipeline:** Integration of multiple sources including official tourism statistics, search trends, and environmental data.
* **Multi-approach Modeling:** Implementation and comparison of various statistical and machine learning models to capture both linear and non-linear patterns.
* **Anomaly Analysis:** Identification and treatment of structural impacts, such as the global pandemic, on regional tourism.
* **Interactive Dashboard:** A narrative-driven interface designed to facilitate the interpretation of results for non-technical stakeholders.
* **Decision Framework:** Practical guidelines based on forecasted demand to optimize resource allocation and pricing strategies.
